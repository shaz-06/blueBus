What is Free Cancellation?

Free Cancellation gives you the freedom to cancel your bus tickets without paying any cancellation charges. Get the full ticket fare refunded with zero penalty till 6 hours before the start of your journey.

How It Works

1

Opt-in for 'Free Cancellation' while booking your bus ticket.

2

Go to 'My Bookings' and cancel your ticket before the cut-off time (6 hours before journey start).

3

Enjoy ZERO penalty and get the full ticket fare instantly refunded.

4

The cancellation charge will be waived off automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I opt-in for free cancellation for my booking?

+

While booking your ticket, you will see an option to add 'Free Cancellation' on the review page before payment. Simply check the box to include it.

2. Can I cancel the free cancellation after purchasing it?

+

No, the 'Free Cancellation' feature is a non-refundable add-on. Once purchased, it cannot be cancelled or refunded, even if you don't use it.

3. What happens to my free cancellation if I reschedule my booking?

+

The 'Free Cancellation' benefit is only valid for the original booking. If you reschedule your ticket, the feature will not be transferred to the new booking and will be forfeited.

4. Why am I still getting cancellation charges even though I have purchased free cancellation?

+

'Free Cancellation' waives charges only if you cancel at least 6 hours before the departure time. If you cancel within the last 6 hours, standard cancellation charges will apply as per the operator's policy.

5. Will my free cancellation be refunded when I cancel my ticket?

+

The fee paid for the 'Free Cancellation' add-on itself is not refundable. However, when you cancel your ticket (within the allowed time), your bus ticket fare will be refunded in full without any deductions.

6. Where will I get my refund on cancellation?

+

The refund will be processed to the original payment method you used while booking. It may take 7-14 business days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank.
