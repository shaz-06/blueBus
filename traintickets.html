Your Journey, Your Train Ticket

Everything you need to know about booking, managing, and using your train ticket with blueBus.

How to Book Your Train Ticket

1. Search

Enter your departure station, destination, travel date, and quota to find available trains.

2. Select

Choose your train, travel class (Sleeper, 3A, 2A, etc.), and check seat availability.

3. Pay

Enter passenger details (ensure names match ID) and complete the payment securely.

4. Confirm

Receive your confirmed e-Ticket and PNR number on the app and in your email.

Understanding Your Train Ticket

Key Information

  • ✓

    PNR Number

    Your 10-digit Passenger Name Record. This is your unique ticket reference for all queries and status checks.

  • ✓

    Ticket Status (CNF / RAC / WL)

    Shows if your seat is Confirmed (CNF), Reservation Against Cancellation (RAC), or Waitlisted (WL).

  • ✓

    Coach & Berth Number

    Your assigned coach (e.g., S1, B2) and seat/berth number (e.g., 32, Lower) if your ticket is CNF or RAC.

  • ✓

    Boarding Station & Time

    The station you must board from and the train's scheduled departure time. Arrive well in advance!

Ticket Types

m-Ticket (Mobile Ticket)

Available in the blueBus app. Show this on your phone with a valid ID. No printout needed.

e-Ticket (Email Ticket)

A PDF copy sent to your email. You can show this PDF on your phone or laptop, along with a valid original ID.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does WL, RAC, and CNF mean?

  • CNF (Confirmed): You have a confirmed seat/berth. Your coach and berth numbers will be shown.
  • RAC (Reservation Against Cancellation): You have a confirmed seat (usually side-lower, shared). You are allowed to board the train. Your seat will likely be upgraded to a full berth if one becomes available.
  • WL (Waitlisted): Your ticket is not confirmed. You cannot board the train with a fully waitlisted e-ticket (an e-ticket that is still WL after the chart is prepared). It gets automatically cancelled, and a refund is processed.

Do I need a printed copy of my e-Ticket?

No. For e-Tickets, you can simply show the m-Ticket in the blueBus app, the e-Ticket PDF on your phone/laptop, or the confirmation SMS sent to your mobile. However, you MUST carry a valid, original photo ID (like Aadhar, Voter ID, Passport, or Driver's License) for verification by the TTE (Travelling Ticket Examiner).

How can I cancel my train ticket?

You can cancel your ticket from the "Manage Booking" section in the blueBus app or website. Cancellation charges are determined by Indian Railways and depend on when you cancel the ticket relative to the train's departure time. Please review the IRCTC cancellation policy for detailed charges.

What if I miss my train?

If you miss your train, it is considered a "No-Show," and you are not eligible for an automatic refund. For certain cases, you may be able to file a TDR (Ticket Deposit Receipt) after the train's departure, but refunds are not guaranteed. We strongly recommend arriving at the station at least 30-60 minutes early.
