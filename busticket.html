Your Journey, Your Ticket

Everything you need to know about booking, managing, and using your bus ticket with blueBus.

How to Book Your Ticket

1. Search

Enter your departure city, destination, and travel date to find available buses.

2. Select

Choose your preferred bus, select your seat, and pick your boarding point.

3. Pay

Enter passenger details and complete the payment using our secure gateway.

4. Confirm

Receive your confirmed m-Ticket on the app and an e-Ticket in your email.

Understanding Your Ticket

Ticket Types

m-Ticket (Mobile Ticket)

Available directly in the blueBus app. It's eco-friendly and convenient. Just show the m-Ticket QR code on your phone to the bus staff.

e-Ticket (Email Ticket)

A PDF copy of your ticket sent to your email. You can either show the PDF on your phone or carry a printout, along with a valid photo ID.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to carry a printed copy of my ticket?

Not necessarily. You can show the m-Ticket in your blueBus app or the e-Ticket PDF on your mobile phone. However, please carry a valid, government-issued photo ID (like an Aadhar card, Voter ID, or Driver's License) for verification.

What ID proof is required during boarding?

You must carry a valid, original photo identification card. The most common acceptable IDs are:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Driver's License
  • Voter ID Card
  • Passport

How can I cancel my bus ticket?

You can cancel your ticket directly from the "Manage Booking" section in the blueBus app or website. Cancellation charges and refund policies vary depending on the bus operator and how close you are to the departure time. Please check the cancellation policy at the time of booking.

What if I miss my bus?

If you miss your bus, you are generally not eligible for a refund. This is considered a "No-Show". We recommend arriving at your boarding point at least 15-30 minutes before the scheduled departure time to avoid this.
