HOW DO I CHOOSE A FLEXI TICKET?

Choose a bus that has a Flexi ticket tag on it.

Look out for this tag when you search for buses

With flexi ticket, you can change your travel date for free up to 8 hours before departure.

Get minimum 50% refund if you cancel your ticket at least 12 hours before the departure.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Free date change means you can reschedule your trip to a future date without any penalty or change fees, as long as it's done within the specified time frame before departure.

With a FlexiTicket, you are guaranteed a minimum refund of 50% if you cancel your ticket at least 12 hours before the scheduled departure time.

When searching for buses, look for the "FlexiTicket" tag next to the bus service listing. You can filter your search to show only buses with this option.

You can check your ticket details in your booking history. If your ticket is a FlexiTicket, it will be clearly mentioned in the ticket information.
